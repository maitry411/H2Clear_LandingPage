What is H₂Clear

As the world races to reduce carbon emissions, green hydrogen emerges as a key clean energy source. However, trust and transparency in certifying truly green hydrogen are critical to unlocking its full potential.


H₂Clear leverages cutting-edge blockchain technology to provide a transparent, immutable, and secure system for the issuance, tracking, and retirement of green hydrogen credits. We help ensure every claim is verifiable and every credit counts toward a cleaner planet.

Who Uses H₂Clear?

Green Hydrogen Producers

  • Request certificates for produced green hydrogen
  • Manage and transfer credits securely

Certifying Authorities

  • Verify production data
  • Approve and issue trusted certificates

Industry Buyers

  • Receive and retire certificates
  • Show compliance with green energy mandates

Regulatory Authorities

  • Audit certificate transactions
  • Monitor for fraud and ensure transparency

Why Choose H₂Clear?

  • Blockchain-Backed Transparency: Every transaction recorded immutably for trust and verifiability.
  • Fraud Prevention: Smart contracts prevent double counting and unauthorized transfers.
  • Easy Integration: Open APIs enable seamless connection with third-party verifiers and government systems.
  • Secure & Scalable: Powered by encryption, trusted authentication, and cloud-native serverless infrastructure.
  • User-Friendly Dashboards: Role-specific insights and controls tailored for each user type.
